Product Description
This three connectors serial bus smart servo possess 35KG torque and adopts all-metal gears inside, strong and durable. It can be controlled by serial port commands, connected to multiple servos at the same time, which can help us create robotic arms and bionic robots. Compared with ordinary servos on the market, it can read back the angle and faceback voltage, temperature position parameter.
